What OBSB Does

OBSB lets you record your screen, microphone, and camera locally in your browser. If you sign in with Google, you may upload recordings to your own Google Drive and create shareable links.

Google Account and Google Drive

  • Sign-in uses your Google account. You grant OBSB permission to access your basic profile (name, email) and to create and manage files in Google Drive that OBSB uploads (drive.file scope — only files created by OBSB).
  • Recordings are stored in your Google Drive account under your control.
  • You can revoke access anytime in your Google Account permissions or by signing out in OBSB.

Your Responsibilities

  • Do not record or share content you do not have the right to record or distribute.
  • Comply with applicable laws and the policies of sites you record.
  • You are responsible for who you share Drive links with.

Privacy

OBSB processes recordings on your device. When you upload to Google Drive, data is sent to Google's services under Google's privacy policy. We do not operate a separate OBSB cloud backend for your videos.
